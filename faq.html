Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is the best time to visit Gran Canaria?

The best time to visit is during the spring and autumn months, when the weather is pleasant and there are fewer tourists. However, I have visited during the winter, which is also a beautiful time of year because of the Christmas decorations.

Do I need a visa to visit Gran Canaria?

It depends on your nationality. Citizens of the EU and many other countries do not need a visa for short stays. Check the specific requirements for your country before traveling.

Travel Questions

How do I get around the island?

Renting a car is a convenient option, but there are also reliable public transportation options such as buses and taxis. I personally took many local buses and walked around, and I really enjoyed getting to know every corner and the people.

Is it safe to travel to Gran Canaria?

Yes, Gran Canaria is a safe destination for travellers both during the day and at night. However, it is always good to take standard precautions and be aware of your surroundings.

What should I pack for my trip?

Pack light, breathable clothing for the warm weather, a light jacket for cooler evenings, comfortable walking shoes, and beach essentials like sunscreen and sunglasses.

Activity Questions

What are the must-see attractions in Gran Canaria?

Some must-see attractions include the Maspalomas Dunes, the historic district of Las Palmas, and the Cathedral of Arucas.

Are there any local festivals I should attend?

Yes, during the winter season there are many local festivals that you can find all over the island.

What outdoor activities are available?

Gran Canaria offers a variety of outdoor activities such as hiking, surfing, snorkeling, and exploring natural pools and volcanic landscapes.
